Subject: Install Heidi App on iPhone (2 minutes)

Hi,

Use this iPhone app link:
https://heidivanhorny.com/inside.html

Install steps (Safari only):
1) Open the link in Safari.
2) Tap the Share icon.
3) Tap "Add to Home Screen".
4) Name it "Heidi HVH" and tap Add.

After that, it works like an app icon on your iPhone home screen.

If it looks outdated, refresh once in Safari and reopen from the home screen icon.
